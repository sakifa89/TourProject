		<h1>Bangladesh at a glance</h1> 
		</center>
		<p>Official Name: The People's Republic of Bangladesh</p>

        <p>State Religion: Islam but other main religions namely Hinduism, Buddhism, Christianity are practiced in peace and harmony.</p>

        <p>State Language: Bangla</p>

        <p>National Anthem: The first ten lines of "Amar Sonar Bangla". written by Nobel Laureate Rabindranath Tagore.</p>

        <p>National Flag: Consists of a circle coloured red throughout its area, resting on a green rectangular background.
		The length to width ratio of the rectangle is 10:6 and the circle has a radius of one fifth of the length.</p>

        <p>National Emblem: The national flower "Shapla" (nymphea-nouchali) resting on water, having on each side an ear of paddy and being surmounted 
		by three connected leaves of jute with two stars on each side of the leaves.</p>

        <p>Capital: Dhaka</p>

        <p>Nationality: Bangladeshi</p>

        <p>Name of Currency: Taka (TK)</p>
         <p>Denominations:
        Notes:Taka 1000, 500, 100, 50, 20, 10, 5, 2 & 1
        Coins: Taka 5, 2, 1 and Paisa 50, 25, 10, 5, 2 & 1</p>

       <p>Geographical Location: Between 20째34' and 26째38' north latitude and between 88째01' and 92째41' east longitude.</p>

       <p>Boundary: North: India
          West: India
          South: Bay of Bengal
          East: India and Myanmar</p>

        <p>Area: 56977 sq. miles or 147570 sq. km.</p>

        <p>Territorial Water: 200 nautical miles.</p>
