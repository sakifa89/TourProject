Money Exchange Point

There are many money exchange companies in Dhaka and other major cities of Bangladesh. People think , to exchange money from Banks is little hassle and getting low rates. Sometime the private or government banks will ask about the legal procedure of earning the foreign currency. But private money broker house will not ask any question to exchange any amount of dollar or Riyals to Bangladesh Taka. On the other hand, private money broker house offer better rates and available when you need. But money changing from broker is a big risk for big amount of foreign currency.
